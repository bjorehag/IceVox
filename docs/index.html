IceVox

Real-time voice effects and P2P voice chat for gaming and roleplay.

Download for Windows

Free — Windows 10/11

PRO TIP

Loopback is fixed at 100% by default so you can instantly hear your magnificent new voice. Make sure to turn it down before joining a room or another voice app to avoid echoing back to your friends!

Features

Voice Effects

Pitch shift, echo, tremolo, vibrato, distortion, chorus, and reverb — 100% customizable.

Character Presets

10 unique character presets + 3 empty slots for your own custom creations.

P2P Voice Chat

Create a room, share the link, and talk. Serverless, zero latency, up to 6 participants.

Opt-in Video Chat

Decoupled P2P video calls via a separate window with adaptive quality.

System-Wide Voice Changer

Routing your voice with VB-Cable turns IceVox into a real-time voice changer for any game, virtual tabletop, or voice chat application. Unleash your inner Orc across the entire internet.
